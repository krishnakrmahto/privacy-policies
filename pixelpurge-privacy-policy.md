# Privacy Policy for PixelPurge

**Last updated:** March 6, 2026

## Overview

PixelPurge ("the App") is a photo library cleaning tool developed by Krishna Kumar ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

**The short version: We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything happens on your device.**

## Information We Access

### Photos & Media Library
The App requires access to your device's photo library to:
- Scan for duplicate and similar photos
- Display photos for review within the App
- Move photos to trash or permanently delete them at your request

**All photo processing happens entirely on your device.** Your photos are never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

### Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` | Read photos from your library for duplicate detection and review |
| `READ_MEDIA_VIDEO` | Read video files from your library for review |
| `ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION` | Access photo metadata (date, location) for organizing by time period |
| `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Legacy permission for devices running Android 12 and below |
| `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Legacy permission for devices running Android 12 and below |

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Photos or media files
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports
- Any data whatsoever to external servers

## Data Storage

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- **Session history**: Statistics about your cleaning sessions (photos reviewed, space saved)
- **Trash queue**: Photos you've moved to trash are copied to the App's private storage and removed from your gallery. They can be restored or permanently deleted from within the App.
- **App preferences**: Settings like auto-delete timer duration
- **Onboarding status**: Whether you've completed the initial setup

This data is stored using your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) and is never transmitted off your device.

## Data Deletion

- You can delete any trashed photos permanently from within the App
- Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored app data
- The App does not retain any data after uninstallation

## Third-Party Services

PixelPurge does **not** use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertising SDKs
- No crash reporting services
- No cloud storage services
- No social media integrations

## Children's Privacy

PixelPurge does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not collect information from any user regardless of age.
